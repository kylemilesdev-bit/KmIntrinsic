About Our Business

Welcome to KM Intrinsic Care, where passion meets craftsmanship. Established in 2022, we've been creating handcrafted candles that bring warmth and comfort to homes across the country.

Our candles are made with premium natural soy wax, lead-free cotton wicks, and carefully selected fragrance oils. Each candle is hand-poured with attention to detail, ensuring the highest quality product for our customers.

We believe in the power of scent to create ambiance, evoke memories, and provide a moment of peace in your busy day. Every candle we create is infused with care and designed to enhance your space.
